Tata Group Data Visualization Simulation

Empowering business with effective data visualizations and insights

Overview

In August 2024, I completed Tata Group's Data Visualization: Empowering Business with Effective Insights simulation on Forage. This program provided practical experience in creating data visualizations and helping executives make informed decisions based on data-driven insights.

The program emphasized the importance of clear communication and impactful data storytelling, enabling me to deliver actionable insights to stakeholders.
